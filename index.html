Miranda Janan

Welcome! My name is Miranda Janan. I'm glad you're here.

On my site, you can learn a little bit about me and the work that I do, view projects and doodles I've created, and leave me a note. In case you’re pressed for time, here’s the quick rundown...

I'm an artist

In most of my free time I’m doing something creative, usually centered around bringing awareness to the Palestinian occupation and diaspora. You can check out some of my work throughout my site.

I'm a social worker

I earned my Master's in Social Work from the University of Michigan in 2017. During the day, I work in public policy research as a form of advocacy for marginalized communities.
